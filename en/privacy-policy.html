Privacy and patient information

Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters, especially when your enquiry involves your health. This policy explains how DentAlba handles personal details, consultation requests, photos, X-rays and other information you choose to share.

Information we collect

When you contact us about dental treatment, we may collect the information you choose to share, including your name, email address, phone or WhatsApp number, treatment interests, consultation requests, and any photos, X-rays, scans, or documents you upload or send to us.

How we use your information

We use this information to respond to your enquiry, arrange consultations, understand the treatment you may be interested in, communicate with you about next steps, and provide patient support before, during, or after your visit.

Some of the information you send may be sensitive medical information. We treat this with extra care and only use it for the purpose of reviewing your request, preparing planning guidance, and coordinating possible provider next steps where appropriate.

Photos, X-rays and documents

If you send photos, X-rays or medical documents, please only upload files that are relevant to your dental enquiry. We recommend removing any information you do not wish to share before uploading.

Online review helps us prepare initial planning guidance, but it is not a final diagnosis, treatment plan, or provider guarantee. Final suitability, price and timing depend on clinical examination and written confirmation by the treating provider.

Sharing and service providers

We do not sell your personal information. We only share your information when needed to provide our services, respond to your request, support secure communication, comply with legal obligations, or work with trusted service providers such as website hosting, secure storage, email, scheduling, or patient communication tools.

International processing

Your information may be processed in Albania and, depending on the tools we use, by service providers in other countries. Where required, we aim to use appropriate safeguards for international transfers of personal data.

Your rights

You may ask us to access, correct, delete, restrict, or object to the use of your personal information, subject to legal, clinical, and administrative obligations. You may also withdraw consent where processing is based on consent.

How long we keep information

We keep personal information only for as long as needed for the purpose it was collected, including enquiry handling, patient coordination, legal compliance, administration, and record-keeping obligations.

Contact

To make a privacy request, contact us at info@dentalba.al.
