Website privacy

Cookie Policy

This page explains how cookies and similar technologies may be used to keep the DentAlba website working, understand visitor activity and support enquiry features.

What cookies do

Our website may use cookies and similar technologies to help the site work properly, understand how visitors use it, improve performance, and support communication or enquiry features.

Types of cookies

Some cookies are essential for the website to function. Others, such as analytics or marketing cookies, should only be used with your consent where required by law.

Cookies may help us understand which pages are useful, whether forms are working correctly, and how patients find us. We do not use cookies to access your medical files or read uploaded documents.

Third-party tools

If third-party tools are used, such as analytics, maps, embedded content, chat, advertising, or booking tools, those providers may set their own cookies according to their policies.

Managing cookies

You can manage or withdraw cookie consent at any time through our cookie settings when available. You can also control cookies through your browser settings.
