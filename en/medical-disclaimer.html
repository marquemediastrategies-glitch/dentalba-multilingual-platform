Clinical limits of online information

Medical Disclaimer

Online information and remote case review can help you prepare questions and understand possible options. They do not replace a clinical examination by a qualified dental professional.

General information only

Information on this website is provided for general educational and patient-support purposes. It is not a diagnosis, medical advice, or a substitute for an in-person examination by a qualified dental professional.

Remote review is preliminary

Every patient is different. Treatment suitability, timing, cost, risks, healing, and expected results can only be assessed after reviewing your medical and dental history, diagnostic records, and, where needed, an in-person clinical examination.

Photos, X-rays, scans, and online consultation requests can help DentAlba understand your situation for planning purposes, but they may not show the full clinical picture. Any preliminary comments or estimates provided remotely are not final treatment plans.

Treatment risks and results

Dental treatment, including implants, veneers, crowns, surgery, whitening, orthodontics, and full-mouth rehabilitation, carries individual risks and limitations. Results cannot be guaranteed.

Urgent symptoms

If you have pain, swelling, bleeding, infection symptoms, trauma, or any urgent medical concern, please seek immediate care from a local dentist, doctor, or emergency service.
