Transparent cost guidance

Understand high-quality dental treatment costs in Albania before you travel.

Albania can offer major savings compared with many UK and Western European private dental fees, while still requiring careful diagnosis, provider review and written planning. Use these examples as cost orientation, not as a final medical quote.

All-on-4 Albania

4,860-7,100 EUR

Illustrative All-on-4 example for one arch, built around 4 implants, 12 crowns and a provisional prosthesis when clinically suitable. Final scope requires diagnostics and provider review.

All-on-6 Albania

6,060 EUR

Starting package for a fixed full-arch restoration where six implants are clinically recommended.

Final pricing can change based on X-rays, bone condition, extractions, grafting, scans, materials, provider terms and the confirmed clinical plan.
