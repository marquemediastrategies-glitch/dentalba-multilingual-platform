Zirconia crowns Albania

Protect and restore broken, weakened or heavily repaired teeth with durable zirconia crowns planned for your bite and smile line.

Ceramic crowns and veneers

Improve the visible smile line with ceramic restorations or veneers when teeth are discoloured, uneven or worn.

Full smile restoration

Combine zirconia, ceramic restorations, veneers and implant work when several teeth need rebuilding for comfort, function and aesthetics.
