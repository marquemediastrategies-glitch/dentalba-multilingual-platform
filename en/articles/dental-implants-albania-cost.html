Cost guide

How much do dental implants cost in Albania?

Dental implant cost depends on the number of implants, crowns, bone condition, materials, scans and whether extra procedures are needed.

Short answer

DentAlba lists All-on-4 at 4860-7100 EUR and All-on-6 at 6060 EUR as package starting points for planning. A personal quote requires X-rays, photos and clinical review by the treating provider.

What is included in the value?

Ask providers about CE-marked implants, an implant passport for future EU follow-up, written guarantee terms, airport pickup, accommodation, and any flight ticket reimbursement conditions.

What affects the final price?

The biggest factors are bone volume, number of missing teeth, extractions, grafting, implant position, crown material, provider terms, and whether the case requires a full-arch restoration.

Why ask for a remote review?

A one-line implant price can be misleading. Sending a panoramic X-ray and photos lets DentAlba prepare more useful planning guidance before you travel.
