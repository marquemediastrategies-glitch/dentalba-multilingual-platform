Private planning review for international patients

Send your dental case before you choose a provider in Albania.

Share your X-rays, photos and treatment goals to receive preliminary guidance, provider-fit notes and estimate direction for implants, All-on-4/6/8, zirconia crowns, veneers, bone grafting, sinus lift or full-mouth restoration.

What to send for a useful answer

Name, country, preferred language, treatment needed, panoramic X-ray if available, teeth photos, preferred travel month, and whether you are travelling with another person. The first answer is preliminary; the final diagnosis and quote must be confirmed by the treating provider after clinical review.

Please only send photos, X-rays or documents that are relevant to your dental enquiry. They are used to prepare planning guidance and contact you about possible treatment routes or provider next steps.
