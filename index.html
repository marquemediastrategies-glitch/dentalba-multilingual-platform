Why costs can be lower

Lower cost does not automatically mean lower clinical quality.

Albania can be more affordable because the economics behind private treatment are different, not because planning should be less careful.

Lower operating costsClinic overhead, property and labor costs are often lower than in the UK and Western Europe.
Coordinated planningRemote case review helps clarify treatment route, provider fit and likely visit timing before travel.
European-standard materialsMaterial choices, lab workflow and CE-certified implant systems should be confirmed in writing.
Quality still needs evidenceThe right comparison is documentation, diagnostics, provider review and aftercare, not price alone.
